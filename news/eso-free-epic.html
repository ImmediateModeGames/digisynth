Elder Scrolls Online is Free on the Epic Games Store

Cameron Patrick 7/20/2023

The acclaimed multiplayer role playing game is currently free on the Epic Games Store from 7/20/2023 until 7/27/2023.

If you play on PC and are a fan of the Elder Scrolls universe go ahead and pickup one of the best MMORPG's available.

Epic is still giving out some top-tier games on their Epic Games Store in the hopes of luring more customers to give the launcher a try and if you have space on you PC, why not jump in? One of the games in the current offering is Elder Scrolls Online(ESO). A sprawling open-world RPG with a lage and active player base, ESO has seen a constant stream of updates and explansions and is now considered one the best MMORPG's available. If you played Skyrim or any other games in the Elder Scrolls series and enjoyed them, there's a rich world of quests and a welcoming community waiting for you in the lands of ESO. Check it out and the other game offerings here and be sure to keep and eye out for a new game every week.

Epic Games Store often gives out free games and has a diverse selection.
